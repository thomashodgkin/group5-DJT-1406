Goals

The purpose of this site is to increase the attendance for barrier reef orchestra concerts, with this focus on their three main concerts. This will be done by by promoting concerts information, such as time, location and events. This benefits the clients by increasing ticket sales.

Success Evaluation

The success evaluation will be done by comparing sales of tickets of the next to three major events/concerts and comparsion to sales 12 months before, allowing for seasonal variance prior to website uploading to after

Target Audience

The target audience will be people 25 and 35 years old, with an interest in classical music.
