Help support your orchestra

The Barrier Reef Orchestra's vision is to touch souls and enrich lives through music. We play a central role of musical life in North Queensland, performing a diverse musical program of concerts throughout each year. The support we receive from individual supporters and corporate sponsors is essential to ensuring we continue provide our community with an inspiring orchestra season now and in the future.

You too can support the Barrier Reef Orchestra by becoming a Corporate Partner or Patron.

Sponsor or donate to the orchestra

The North Queensland Ensembles Inc is a tax deductible fund listed on the Register of Cultural Organisations under Subdivision 30-B of the Income tax Assessment Act 1997. Donations of $2.00 or more are tax deductible. Help us continue to provide quality entertainment for the Townsville community by supporting the orchestra.
